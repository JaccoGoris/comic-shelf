# Comic Management

Comic Shelf lets you build and manage a complete digital catalogue of your comic book collection.

## Adding Comics

Comics can be added in several ways:

1. **Manual entry** — fill in title, issue number, series, publisher, and cover image
2. **UPC Barcode lookup** — scan or enter a UPC to auto-populate metadata from the [Metron API](/features/metron-integration)
3. **Barcode scanner** — use the built-in [QuaggaJS scanner](/features/barcode-scanner)

## Browsing & Filtering

- Filter by series, publisher, or read/unread status
- Paginated comic list with cover thumbnails
- Detail panel (draggable) showing full metadata

## Editing & Deleting

- Edit any field on existing comics
- Delete with a confirmation dialog (no accidental deletions)
- Cover images can be uploaded directly or fetched from Metron
